# Eggs in Tomato Sauce

Version: 1.0  
Status: To test
Icon: 🍅

## Summary

Three eggs gently cooked in a reduced tomato sauce, with bread for the inevitable scarpetta.

## Time

25 minutes

## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 200-250 ml passata
- 1/2 onion
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
- Chilli flakes
- Black pepper
- Oregano
- 1 slice bread

## Method

1. Soften the onion in oil, adding water if needed.
2. Add garlic for 30 seconds.
3. Add passata, chilli, pepper and oregano.
4. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
5. Make three wells and crack in the eggs.
6. Cover and cook for five to seven minutes.
7. Serve with bread.
