# Mushroom Risotto

Version: 1.0  
Status: To test

## Summary

A proper risotto finished with Parmesan and a spoon of stock instead of butter.

## Time

35 minutes

## Ingredients

- 70 g arborio rice
- 150 g chestnut mushrooms, sliced
- 1/2 onion, finely chopped
- 1 garlic clove
- 500 ml hot vegetable stock
- 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
- 15 g Parmesan, grated
- Black pepper
- Fresh parsley

## Method

1. Brown the mushrooms first and set them aside.
2. Soften the onion in the oil, adding splashes of water as needed.
3. Add the garlic for 30 seconds, then the rice for one minute.
4. Add the hot stock a ladle at a time, stirring often.
5. Return the mushrooms for the last five minutes.
6. Off the heat, beat in the Parmesan with a final spoon of stock.
7. Rest for two minutes, then finish with parsley and pepper.

## Notes

- The final beating with Parmesan and stock replaces the usual butter.
- The stock must be hot; cold stock stalls the cooking.
