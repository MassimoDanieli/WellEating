# Harissa Shakshuka

Version: 1.0  
Status: To test

## Summary

The eggs-in-tomato formula pushed towards North Africa: harissa, cumin, red pepper and cooling yoghurt.

## Time

30 minutes

## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 200-250 ml passata
- 1/2 onion
- 1/2 red pepper, sliced
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 teaspoon harissa
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
- 1 tablespoon Greek yoghurt
- Fresh parsley
- 1 slice of bread

## Method

1. Soften the onion and pepper in the oil, with splashes of water.
2. Add the garlic for 30 seconds, then harissa and cumin.
3. Add the passata and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
4. Make three wells and crack in the eggs.
5. Cover and cook for five to seven minutes.
6. Top with yoghurt and parsley; serve with the bread.

## Notes

- The yoghurt balances the harissa, as in the couscous.
- Same base as eggs in tomato sauce; the spices do the travelling.
