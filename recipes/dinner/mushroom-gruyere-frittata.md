# Mushroom and Gruyère Frittata

Version: 1.0  
Status: To test

## Summary

The frittata formula with browned mushrooms, thyme and Gruyère.

## Time

25 minutes

## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 150 g chestnut mushrooms, sliced
- 1/2 onion, thinly sliced
- 25 g Gruyère, grated
- 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
- Thyme
- Black pepper

## Method

1. Brown the mushrooms in the oil and set aside.
2. Soften the onion in the same pan, using splashes of water.
3. Return the mushrooms with the thyme.
4. Beat the eggs with pepper and most of the Gruyère.
5. Pour in, lower the heat, cover and cook until nearly set.
6. Flip briefly, or grill only if the pan is oven-safe.

## Notes

- Same structure as the courgette frittata; only the filling changes.
- Brown the mushrooms properly first or they will water down the eggs.
