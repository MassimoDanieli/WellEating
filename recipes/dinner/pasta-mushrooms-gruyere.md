# Pasta with Mushrooms and Gruyère

Version: 1.0  
Status: To test

## Summary

Browned chestnut mushrooms, a silky pasta-water sauce and Gruyère added off the heat.

## Time

25 minutes

## Ingredients

- 75 g short pasta
- 200 g chestnut mushrooms, thickly sliced
- 1 garlic clove
- 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
- 25 g Gruyère, grated
- Fresh parsley
- Black pepper

## Method

1. Cook the pasta; keep a cup of the water.
2. Brown the mushrooms in the oil over high heat without crowding the pan.
3. Add the garlic for 30 seconds.
4. Add the drained pasta with a splash of pasta water and toss.
5. Off the heat, stir in the Gruyère until it melts into the sauce.
6. Finish with parsley and black pepper.

## Notes

- Gruyère goes stringy if boiled; always add it off the heat.
- Mushrooms brown properly only if the pan is not crowded.
