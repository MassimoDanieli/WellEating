# Pasta with Courgette and Lemon

Version: 1.0  
Status: To test

## Summary

Courgette cooked down until almost creamy, lifted with lemon and Parmesan.

## Time

25 minutes

## Ingredients

- 75 g short pasta
- 1 large courgette, half thinly sliced, half grated
- 1 garlic clove
- Zest and juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
- 15 g Parmesan, grated
- Black pepper
- Fresh basil, optional

## Method

1. Cook the pasta; keep a cup of the water.
2. Cook the sliced courgette in the oil until golden.
3. Add the grated courgette and garlic; cook until soft and almost creamy.
4. Add the pasta with a splash of its water and the lemon zest.
5. Off the heat, add the Parmesan and lemon juice.
6. Finish with pepper and basil.

## Notes

- The grated half melts into a sauce; the sliced half keeps texture.
- Lemon juice off the heat only, or it turns bitter.
