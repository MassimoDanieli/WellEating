# Zucchini, Caramelised Onion and Emmental Frittata

Version: 1.1  
Status: Production ready

## Summary

Caramelised onion, tender courgette and enough cheese to make added salt optional.

## Rating

★★★★★

## Time

25 minutes

## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 courgette, thinly sliced
- 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
- 30-40 g Emmental, grated
- 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
- 1/2 teaspoon honey
- Black pepper
- Very little salt, or none

## Method

1. Heat the oil over medium heat.
2. Cook the onion for four minutes.
3. Add the honey and continue cooking gently.
4. Add a tablespoon of water whenever the pan becomes too dry.
5. Add the courgette and cook until tender but not mushy.
6. Beat the eggs with black pepper and most of the Emmental.
7. Pour into the pan and lower the heat.
8. Cover and cook until nearly set.
9. Turn briefly, or finish under a grill only if the pan is oven-safe.

## Notes

- Good hot and cold.
- The caramelised onion and cheese make added salt largely unnecessary.
- Water works better than extra oil when the onion starts catching.
