# Greek Yoghurt with Oats, Honey and Fruit

Version: 1.0  
Status: To test

## Summary

A five-minute breakfast with protein, slow carbs and just enough honey.

## Time

5 minutes

## Ingredients

- 150 g Greek yoghurt
- 30 g oats
- 1 teaspoon honey
- A handful of berries or half a banana
- Cinnamon
- Optional few walnuts

## Method

1. Stir the oats into the yoghurt.
2. Rest for five minutes, or overnight in the fridge.
3. Top with the fruit, honey and cinnamon.

## Notes

- Overnight, the oats soften completely and it eats like a dessert.
- Measure the honey; it is the easiest place for sugar to creep.
