# Avocado and Egg on Toast

Version: 1.0  
Status: To test

## Summary

Wholemeal toast, half an avocado and one properly cooked egg.

## Time

10 minutes

## Ingredients

- 1 slice wholemeal bread
- 1/2 ripe avocado
- 1 egg
- Juice of 1/4 lemon
- Chilli flakes
- Black pepper

## Method

1. Toast the bread.
2. Mash the avocado with the lemon juice and pepper.
3. Poach or fry the egg with minimal oil.
4. Assemble and finish with chilli flakes.

## Notes

- Half an avocado is the portion; the other half keeps with its stone and lemon.
- The lemon is not optional; it lifts the whole thing.
