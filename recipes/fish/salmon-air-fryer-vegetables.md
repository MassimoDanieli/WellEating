# Air-Fryer Salmon with Vegetables

Version: 1.1  
Status: Tested

## Summary

A simple working-day dinner with crisp vegetables and properly juicy salmon.

## Time

20 minutes

## Ingredients

- 180-200 g salmon fillet
- Courgette
- Red pepper
- Onion
- Up to 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil for the full dish
- Black pepper
- Lemon
- Optional smoked paprika

## Method

1. Cut vegetables into fairly thick pieces.
2. Start the vegetables first at 190 C.
3. Add the salmon later so it cooks for only eight to ten minutes.
4. Remove the salmon as soon as it is just cooked; residual heat will continue the process.
5. Finish with lemon and black pepper.

## Notes

- Thin courgette slices overcook quickly.
- The salmon was best removed slightly before the timer ended.
