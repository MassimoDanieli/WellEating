# Dijon, Honey and Lemon Tofu

Version: 1.1  
Status: Tested

## Ingredients

- 180-200 g super-firm tofu
- 2 teaspoons smooth Dijon mustard
- 1 teaspoon honey
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
- Black pepper
- Chilli flakes
- Optional thyme or oregano

## Method

1. Cut the tofu into two-centimetre cubes.
2. Mix the marinade ingredients.
3. Coat the tofu and leave for 15-20 minutes.
4. Air fry at 190 C for eight minutes.
5. Check immediately; super-firm tofu dries quickly.
6. Add only one or two further minutes if needed.

## Notes

- M&S super-firm tofu was already dry at nine minutes.
- Use smooth Dijon, not wholegrain, for a proper coating.
