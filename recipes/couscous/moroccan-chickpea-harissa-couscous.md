# Moroccan-Style Chickpea and Harissa Couscous

Version: 1.1  
Status: Tested

## Summary

Warm, spicy couscous balanced with lemon, parsley and Greek yoghurt.

## Time

25 minutes

## Ingredients

- 80 g dry couscous, prepared and fluffed
- 1 medium onion, sliced
- 120 g chickpeas, drained
- Cherry tomatoes, when available
- 1 teaspoon harissa, adjusted to taste
- 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
- 1/2 teaspoon honey
- 1/2 lemon
- 1 small garlic clove
- Fresh parsley
- 1-2 tablespoons Greek yoghurt
- Black pepper

## Method

1. Cook the onion in one teaspoon of oil.
2. Add small splashes of water to prevent burning.
3. Add honey after about four minutes.
4. Add garlic for 30 seconds.
5. Add chickpeas and cook for two or three minutes.
6. Stir in the harissa and a little water.
7. Add tomatoes briefly, if using.
8. Fold in the couscous.
9. Finish with lemon, parsley and black pepper.
10. Serve with Greek yoghurt on top.

## Notes

- The yoghurt balances the harissa.
- Do not rely on oil alone to cook the onion; water prevents scorching.
