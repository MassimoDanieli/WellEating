# Couscous with Salmon, Lemon and Herbs

Version: 1.0  
Status: To test

## Summary

Air-fryer salmon flaked through bright, herby couscous.

## Time

20 minutes

## Ingredients

- 80 g dry couscous, prepared and fluffed
- 150 g salmon fillet
- 1 spring onion, sliced
- Zest and juice of 1/2 lemon
- Fresh parsley and dill
- 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
- Black pepper

## Method

1. Air fry the salmon at 190 C for eight to ten minutes.
2. Prepare the couscous with the lemon zest.
3. Flake the salmon into the couscous.
4. Add the spring onion, herbs, oil and lemon juice.
5. Finish with black pepper.

## Notes

- Same salmon timing as the air-fryer salmon recipe: remove it slightly early.
- The zest goes in the couscous water; the juice goes in at the end.
