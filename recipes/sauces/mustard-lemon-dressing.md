# Mustard and Lemon Dressing

Version: 1.1  
Status: Production ready

## Summary

A bright everyday dressing that works with salads, fish and warm vegetables.

## Time

3 minutes

## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon smooth Dijon mustard
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
- Black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon honey, optional
- Chives, optional

## Method

1. Whisk until emulsified.
2. Add a teaspoon of water if a thinner dressing is needed.

## English mustard variation

Use only 1/2 teaspoon English mustard and include honey to soften the heat.
