# Bresaola, Rocket and Parmesan Plate

Version: 1.0  
Status: Tested
Icon: 🥩

## Summary

Minimal preparation, strong flavours and a sensible portion of bread on the side.

## Time

8 minutes

## Ingredients

- 80 g bresaola
- Rocket
- 20 g Parmesan
- Cherry tomatoes
- 1 slice wholemeal bread
- 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
- Lemon
- Black pepper

## Method

1. Arrange the rocket and bresaola.
2. Add shaved Parmesan and tomatoes.
3. Finish with oil, lemon and black pepper.
4. Serve with wholemeal bread.
