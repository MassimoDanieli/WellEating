# Tuna and Mustard Salad

Version: 1.0  
Status: Tested

## Ingredients

- Mixed salad leaves or rocket
- 80 g tinned tuna, drained
- Thinly sliced onion or spring onion
- Olives
- Chickpeas, optional
- Sauerkraut, optional
- 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 teaspoon English mustard
- 1/2 teaspoon honey, optional
- Black pepper

## Method

1. Combine the salad ingredients.
2. Whisk the oil, lemon, mustard, honey and pepper.
3. Dress immediately before eating.
