# French Onion Soup with Gruyère

Version: 1.0  
Status: To test

## Summary

Slow caramelised onions using the water-splash technique, topped with a Gruyère toast.

## Time

50 minutes

## Ingredients

- 2 large onions, thinly sliced
- 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
- 1/2 teaspoon honey
- 500 ml beef or vegetable stock
- Optional splash of white wine
- 1 slice wholemeal bread
- 30 g Gruyère, grated
- Black pepper
- Thyme

## Method

1. Cook the onions slowly in the oil for 20-25 minutes.
2. Add small splashes of water whenever they start to catch.
3. Add the honey halfway through.
4. Deglaze with the wine, if using.
5. Add the stock and thyme; simmer for 15 minutes.
6. Toast the bread, top with Gruyère and grill until melted.
7. Serve the soup with the toast on top and black pepper.

## Notes

- Same onion technique as the frittata, just scaled up and slower.
- The onions decide the whole soup; do not rush the first stage.
