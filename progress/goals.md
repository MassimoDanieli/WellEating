# Goals

## Primary goal

Move gradually from 100 kg towards 90 kg while keeping food enjoyable.

## Milestones

- 97.5 kg
- 95 kg
- 92.5 kg
- 90 kg

## Behaviour goals

- Cook most meals at home.
- Use water as the main drink.
- Cycle when weather and schedule allow.
- Avoid wasting fresh food.
- Keep pizza and beer as normal occasional foods, not failures.
